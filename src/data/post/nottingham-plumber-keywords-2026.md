You don’t need hundreds of keywords to get leads.

You need the **right** keywords — the ones with clear intent — and pages that make it easy to call.

Below is a practical keyword list for **Nottingham plumbers**, based on how people actually search for local trades:

- service + location
- emergency intent
- specific problems (leaks, blocked drains)
- boiler/heating terms
- “near me” searches

> Note: keyword tools will show different volumes. The point here is the *structure* and how to turn these terms into pages that convert.

## 2) Location modifiers (Nottingham areas)
These are powerful because people often search by suburb.

Use the same structure:
- **[service] + [area]**

Examples:
- plumber west bridgford
- plumber beeston
- plumber arnold nottingham
- plumber mapperley
- plumber hucknall
- plumber wollaton

Even if you don’t make a page for every area, include an **Areas We Cover** section listing them.

---

## 3) “Near me” keywords (how to capture them)
You can’t put “near me” everywhere, but you can rank for it with good local signals.

- plumber near me
- emergency plumber near me
- boiler repair near me

To capture these, you need:
- Google Business Profile set up properly
- consistent NAP (name/address/phone)
- service area + locations mentioned on-page
- reviews
